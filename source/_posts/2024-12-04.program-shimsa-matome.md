### 英語版
> &emsp;Aituan Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2015. It is an innovative enterprise focused on community group buying, dedicated to providing convenient and cost-effective daily necessities and fresh produce for community residents. Guided by the philosophy of "neighborly assistance and shared better living," the company integrates supply chain resources to deliver high-quality products directly to communities. This approach reduces intermediaries, enabling low prices, high efficiency, and fast delivery. Leveraging a digital platform, Aituan Technology offers users a one-stop group buying experience. The platform covers a wide range of product categories, including fruits and vegetables, meat and poultry, snacks, and daily necessities. The company's goal is to provide every family with a more affordable and convenient shopping experience.
　The Community Group Buying Comprehensive Management System Program was initiated with the approval of the Board of Directors of Aituan Technology Co., Ltd. It is a key strategic project aimed at supporting the company's rapid growth and development.  The system platform provides a range of functions, including group buying order management, real-time supply chain monitoring, online application and assessment for community leaders, live streaming, and chat features. By integrating these functions, the platform significantly enhances operational efficiency, reduces communication and time costs, and speeds up response times. This supports the company's efforts to achieve efficient collaboration and sustainable development in the community group buying sector, creating greater value for community residents and business partners.
　Program Strategic Objectives:
　　1. By December 2020, increase the number of users to 2,000,000 (±10%).
　　2. By December 2020, raise the total monthly transaction volume of goods to 30 million USD (±10%).
　　3. By December 2019, shorten the average delivery time to 8 hours (±30 minutes).
　　4. By December 2020, reduce the proportion of system maintenance costs to 35% (±5%).

　　4. Regularly report program progress to the Program Steering Committee. This includes key deliverables, potential risks, mitigation measures, and required support. Ensure senior management stays informed and has the data needed for decision-making.
　　5. Manage, monitor, and track the overall benefits of the program. Ensure the realization of expected outcomes, such as increased transaction volume and reduced operating costs, to maximize program benefits during project implementation.

　　4. 变更管理机制：为了确保变更过程的透明管理，避免无序变更对项目集的负面影响，我设计了一套变更管理机制。

　　案例1

　　　　风险：增加新的子项目可能导致原项目集的时间和资源分配受到冲击，特别是对千帆系统的性能优化计划产生冲击。
　　　　成本：新增“买菜系统”的成本预测比预期高出10%，但预期收益（包括新增用户数和交易额）将大幅提升，覆盖成本的可能性高。

　　　　决策与审批：治理委员会在全面评估变更的可行性后，批准了该变更请求。
　　　　实施与监控：我对项目资源进行了重新分配，调整了里程碑时间表，确保“买菜系统”开发与原有项目集的同步推进。
　　　变更结果：
　　　　通过该变更，“买菜系统”成功上线，帮助公司在疫情期间迅速占领市场，用户量和订单交易额大幅增长，为公司带来了显著的战略和经济收益。
